HEALTH CHECK PACKAGES

CAREMATRIX Hospital presents a range of health check packages. These preventive health check packages are custom designed for different age groups & disease profiles.

WHEN YOU ARE COMING FOR HEALTH CHECKUP

  1. On appearing for a test, be in fasting for 10 hours. You can drink water
  2. Please register at the counter between 6.30 am to 8.00 am
  3. Please keep your routine medicines and insulin with you and take them after giving blood sample
  4. Please do not increase or decrease the intake of your breakfast. Have it as normal as you do
  5. Eye examination, dental examination and gynecology consultation will be between 10.30 am and 12 pm. Please report at the station concerned on time.
  6. Test results can be collected only by evening. You should come prepared to stay back in the hospital till the results are delivered
