CAREMATRIX Hospital presents a range of health check packages. These preventive health check packages are custom designed for different age groups & disease profiles.

When You Are Coming for Health Checkup

  1. Be in fasting for 10 hours before your test. You can drink water.
  2. Please register at the counter between 6:30 am and 8:00 am.
  3. Bring your routine medicines and insulin, and take them after giving the blood sample.
  4. Do not change your breakfast routine; have it as usual after the tests.
  5. Eye, dental, and gynecology consultations are between 10:30 am and 12:00 pm. Please be punctual.
  6. Test results will be available by evening. Please plan to stay until then.
