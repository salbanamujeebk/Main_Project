ABOUT SMK

The vision of ‘affordable, accessible, and quality healthcare for all’, a dream of Dr. K G Alexander, became a reality in 1987 with the commencement of Baby Memorial Hospital (BMH). The initiative sparked a revolution in the healthcare segment in Kerala, marking a new era of healthcare excellence. A tribute to late K C Varghese (known as ‘Baby’), father of Dr. K G Alexander, the hospital has set new benchmarks for care. Vision & Mission

Vision: Affordable, Accessible, and Quality Services to All.

Mission: “BMH provides quality healthcare in a safe and patient-centered environment by highly skilled professionals, using advanced technology and best practices. Our services offer value in an eco-friendly atmosphere.”

CAREMATRIX - More Than Care

Our commitment to care for three decades has led us to better understand how to offer you more than just healthcare. By combining medical expertise with compassion, we provide a unique healing experience tailored to your needs.
